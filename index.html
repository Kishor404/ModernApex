| Our Works

ESU1-DUSP Dismantling

At Modern Apex Electromechanical Work LLC, we offer specialized dismantling services for electrical and utility systems, including the ESU1-DUSP (Electrical Substation Unit 1 - Distribution and Utility Support Panel). Our team of experts ensures that every dismantling project is executed with precision, safety, and minimal disruption to your operations.

MCCB Modification

We specialize in the modification and upgrading of Molded Case Circuit Breakers (MCCBs) to ensure optimal performance, safety, and compliance with the latest industry standards. Our expert team provides comprehensive MCCB modification services tailored to meet the specific needs of your electrical systems.

Feeder Pillar

We offer top-notch Feeder Pillar solutions tailored to meet the specific needs of your electrical distribution system. Our comprehensive services ensure the safe and efficient distribution of electrical power, enhancing the reliability and performance of your network.
