| Quality Policy

The management of Modern Apex Electromechanical Works LLC has defined and documented its Quality Policy. This policy includes the organization’s commitment for meeting customer requirements and for continual improvement.

Quality Policy Statement.:

  • The goal of Modern Apex Electromechanical Works LLC's quality policy is to consistently deliver excellent quality in all of our services. We are always conscious of the fact that the services we provide are extremely important to our clients and that both their safety and that of our personnel.
  • Our commitment is to the accuracy and safety of our customers' or their professional who directly interface with what we offer or serve, in their facility.

| Health, Safety And Environment Policy

  • All work is completed in accordance with Modern Apex Electromechanical Works LLC's Quality Management System (QMS), the company's applicable technical and administrative operational policies and procedures, and any applicable legal, regulatory, and particular customer requirements.
  • We will keep enhancing our staff and procedures to foresee, meet, and surpass the expectations of our clients with the help of input from the front lines and management leadership.
  • We support the continually improving quality of our customer's maintenance and other technical operations through the services we provide. The quality policy and quality objectives are relevant to the department’s organizational goals and the expectations and needs of its customers. These concepts have been communicated to all employees within the scope of the QMS.
  • Modern Apex Electromechanical Works LLC is conscious of the importance to protect the Health, Safety and Environment of its all employees and as such it is our policy to execute work favouring all who work within the confines of the company.
  • In order to attain this objective Modern Apex Electromechanical Works LLC implements an elaborated Health, Safety and Environmental plan for the execution of work within an operating plant.
  • The responsibility in administering this plan covers all from top management to project management to the employees working at sites.
  • All activities are to be performed in complete compliance with the rules and regulations established by the project management and in overall compliance with the standard Health, Safety and Environmental in the work premises abiding local statutory rules and regulations.
  • Management is committed to ensure that Health, Safety and Environmental policy shall be implemented throughout the organization. Without the personal commitment and active participation by all in this plan our objective of “ZERO ACCIDENTS” will be unattainable.
  • Our Health, Safety and Environmental policy will also be applicable to our sub-contractors and suppliers and our project management is committed to abide with all rules and norms while at working on site.
